## Tips for Evaluating Sources
- Look for author credentials and institutional affiliations
- Check for citations and sources
- Verify publication dates (especially important for current events)
- Cross-reference multiple trustworthy sources
- Avoid sites with excessive ads or clickbait
- Be skeptical of sites ending in .ai, .blog, or other commercial TLDs pushing content
